Just a Friend

Release Date: January 1, 2000

Genre: R&B / Soul

Label: Rock Records

Song Overview

“Just a Friend” is one of David Tao’s signature works, featured on the album I'm OK. With a tender melody and nuanced emotion, the song explores the complexity of a relationship. Blending R&B and soul, his expressive vocal delivery turns layered feelings into a Mandopop classic.
