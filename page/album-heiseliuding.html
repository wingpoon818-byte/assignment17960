Black Tangerine

Release Date: August 9, 2002

Genre: R&B / Rock / Soul

Label: EMI

Song Overview

“Black Tangerine” is the title track of David Tao’s third album and one of his most experimental works. With vivid imagery and reflective lyrics, it explores loneliness and confusion in modern urban life. Blending R&B, rock, and soul, the song showcases his creative depth. The “black tangerine” image suggests a dark exterior that still holds inner hope.
