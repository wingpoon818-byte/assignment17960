Catherine

Release Date: August 9, 2002

Genre: R&B / Jazz / Soul

Label: EMI

Song Overview

“Catherine,” from the album Black Tangerine, is a moving love ballad named after a person. It tells a haunting love story, delivered with David Tao’s gentle, emotive vocals over a jazz‑infused arrangement. The blend of R&B, jazz, and soul creates a romantic yet bittersweet atmosphere that highlights his distinctive songwriting.
