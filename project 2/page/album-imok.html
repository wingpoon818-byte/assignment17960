I'm OK

Release Date: December 10, 1999

Genre: R&B / Pop

Label: Rock Records

Song Overview

“I'm OK” is the title track from David Tao’s second album and a showcase of his growing maturity as a songwriter. With an upbeat groove and optimistic tone, it expresses a positive outlook in the face of life’s challenges. The song carries his signature R&B style while adding more pop elements for a warm, healing feel.
