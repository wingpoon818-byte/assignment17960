Introduction

David Tao (born July 11, 1969, in Hong Kong)

A pioneer of Mandopop R&B—singer, songwriter, producer—who reshaped the sound and storytelling of Chinese pop music.

Early Life and Education (1969–1992)

- Artistic family: his father Tao Dawei was a renowned Taiwanese entertainer, and his mother a Peking opera performer. He studied at Bethany American School in Taipei and moved to California at age 15.
- Universities: attended UC Irvine and UCLA, majoring in psychology and minoring in film.
- Diverse experiences: worked as a tutor, music store clerk, film extra (Black Rain), served 18 months as an LAPD officer, then joined a film company.

Behind-the-Scenes Breakthrough (1993–1996)

- Spring 1993: met producer Wang Zhiping at a Los Angeles music store, returned to Taiwan, and began a production career.
- 1994: produced Jeff Chang’s English album Somewhere In My Broken Heart and wrote/produced for artists like Sarah Chen, Winnie Hsin, Ronald Cheng, building strong industry credibility.

On-Stage Peak (1997–2014)

- 1997: released the debut album David Tao. Tracks like Airport at 10:30 Beach Simple Love ignited the Mandopop R&B wave and won Golden Melody awards for Best New Artist and Best Album Producer.
- 2000: the album I'm OK won Best Album Producer at the 11th Golden Melody Awards, while Just a Friend became a massive hit.
- 2002: Black Tangerine blended rock, jazz, and social themes, cementing his creative peak and classic status.
- 2006: So Beautiful sold two million copies across Asia; in 2007 he performed Today You Will Marry Me on the CCTV Spring Festival Gala, winning Song of the Year at the Golden Melody Awards.
- 2010: won Best Mandarin Male Singer at the 21st Golden Melody Awards with 69 Chapter, marking a major career milestone.
- 2013: launched the Little People Rhapsody world tour and expanded his reach through variety shows.

Legacy and Style

- Pioneered the fusion of R&B with Mandopop, Chinese elements, and rock, paving the way for later artists and earning the title “Godfather of Mandopop R&B.”
- Signature works: Simple Love Just a Friend Black Tangerine Today You Will Marry Me The Season of Solitude and more, combining popularity with artistic value.
